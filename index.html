JELLY PUDDINGS

Jelly puddings are loved by consumers in many countries, especially in spring and summer, when the lovely shape and sweet taste of jelly make people feel refreshing. Nowadays, young consumers prefer online shopping and rarely go to offline physical stores, so a jelly pudding sales website is set up to facilitate everyone to buy, and you can enjoy a 10% discount!

Want to know more →
Poster

The poster was made in GIMP and also meant to be promotional with a 10% discount to attract customers; similarly, most of the principles applied in its creation matched and followed the same ideas and principles as the banner. The primary goal was to make the design look professional as well as make the colors look vibrant and the images to look as clear as possible which were the image retouching techniques employed.

Logo

The logo for the project reflects the theme of the jelly puddings, which are fun and not very serious. It has employed a jelly shape icon and a rounded, simple font that looked more like a creamy sweet dessert. The colors or pastel pink and blue used in the logo emphasized the dessert concept and the straightforward and clear style of the logo made the logo adaptable for a variety of purposes. The logo was placed on the site and in product images to strengthen the positioning of brand associations and the link with the target audience.

BANNER

To satisfy the target audience, an eye-catching banner image was created to inform people about the ongoing sale. The banner design displayed huge and conspicuous text in contrast colors to ensure the message conveyed gets out.

Video Motion

The motion involved in depicting the jelly was the wobbling movement, which made the users get the feel of the product as they were using it. Each mode to the geometry to the texturing and even animation was done to make sure that the final result had a magnificent look while still closely resembling the real product.

Storyboard

In order to explain the multimedia components used within the project, the storyboard was used to describe the various uses of multimedia in the platform. A stripped-down website interface was intended, consisting of various high-resolution images of jelly puddings in a slider. Each flavor was clearly stamped along with its corresponding shape; brief descriptions indicated that customers could compare one flavor to another and one shape to another.
